Dungeons and Dragons

The Greatest Table-Top Role-Playing Game Ever Made

Dungeons and Dragons was first made in 1974 and has had 5 different versions over the years.
In the 2021 celebration, it was announced that 5.5e or 6e will be released in 2024.
D&D has been a massive influence on the whole world when it introduced role playing games.
D&D requires 2 types of people, a DM and the party. The DM discribes and "controls" the game while the party plays it.
